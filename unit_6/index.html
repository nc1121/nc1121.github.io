OUR MISSION

New England Dog Rescue is dedicated to saving abandoned, neglected, and at-risk dogs from high-kill shelters and finding them loving homes in the New England area.

Adoption Program

We save lives and create happy tails by matching adoptable dogs with their forever homes.

Training & Medical Care

Dogs in our care receive a full medical evaluation, necessary treatment, and basic training.

Community Outreach

Our pet resource center provides health services for low-income pet owners in the community.

FOSTERS NEEDED!

Opening your home to a dog in need through our foster program is an incredibly rewarding experience. Our program covers all expenses needed for the care of your foster pup.
